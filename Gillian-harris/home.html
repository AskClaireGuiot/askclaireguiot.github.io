Uncommon Spirit Co

Uncommon Spirit Co is located in Calgary, Alberta and focuses on creating jewelry, accessories and lifestyle products.

They use mainly brass, copper, aluminum and stainless steel.

Items can be personalized using the technique of metal stamping where each character is hammered into the metal to make a lasting impression.
