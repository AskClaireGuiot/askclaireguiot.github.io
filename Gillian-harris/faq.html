I highly reccommend that you do not shower with your jewelry on. Please put your jewelry on last, after all lotions and perfumes to preserve it's quality.



Brass and copper are natural minerals and therefore react with the body's natural oils and anything else applied (makeup, lotion, perfume). They will oxidize (change color and get darker) and may turn your skin green as a reaction with your skin's pH level. This is completely normal, and not dangerous. Returns are not provided for this reason.

Brass

Brass can be cleaned using Brasso and a soft cloth. You can also use ketchup or soy sauce. A polishing cloth can help add extra shine.

Copper

The copper is not coated and will darken over time. To return it to a bright pink color simply soak it in a dish of vinegar for a few minutes, rinse and dry fully. You can also use a polishing cloth.

Mixed Items

Please use a soft cloth to keep your item clean. If you have any questions about the materials used in your item please email me!
