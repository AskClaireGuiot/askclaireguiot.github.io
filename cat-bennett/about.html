About AngelMaker

Band Bio

Genre

Melodic deathcore

Location

North Vancouver, BC, Canada

Year formed

2011

Band summary

AngelMaker levels listeners with a furious, diverse, and violent extremity.

Across a handful of split EPs and other releases, including two full-length albums, the North Vancouver six-piece make their musical intentions clear.
AngelMaker creates deathcore of the highest order, injected with blackened thrash, a smattering of power violence, and the urgent ferocity of underground hardcore.
The band's dual-vocal and triple-guitar composition allows for new and diverse explorations of extremity, setting AngelMaker both inside and apart from the traditions of the deathcore subgenre.
