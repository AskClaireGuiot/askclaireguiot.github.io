Brenda's Glass Expressions

Brenda has been owner and operator for sixteen years now, time flies when you're having fun. Brenda and her sister Donna work together to provide the exceptional customer care that Glass Expressions is known for. We carry a wide selection of stained glass, tools and supplies, to help ensure that you will have everything to successfully complete your projects.

If you are close to our store, please feel free to drop in and talk with us.

On the Comox estuary, fixed between the busy Comox road and where the wild ocean meets a river; sits a glass studio … next to an old but well-used boat launch … and a field of wildflowers … where brothers park houses on stilts … it's a remarkable place … a crux of wild and old and useful and creative and powerful. But it never overstates itself, this place. It's rather unassuming … but remarkable.

Cindy Trevitt-Comox Valley Artist
