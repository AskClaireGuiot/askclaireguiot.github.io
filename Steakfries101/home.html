Welcome to Glass Expressions!

Glass Expressions is proudly veteran owned and the "go to" place for glass working tools and supplies on Northern Vancouver Island. The store is located in the beautiful Comox Valley, British Columbia, Canada, home of the prestigious Filberg Festival of the Arts.

​ Brenda has been owner and operator for sixteen years now....Time flies when you're having fun!! She and her sister Donna work together to provide the exceptional customer care that Glass Expressions is known for.

If you are close to our store please feel free to drop in and talk with us.
​
​ Jan Lindstrom's gallery and studio, Jan's Glass by the Sea, is open right across the street. Jan gladly accepts custom glass art orders or she will teach you the skills to create your own
