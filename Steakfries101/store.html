Our Store

Glass Expressions is a retail outlet for a good variety of supplies, tools and glass. If you can visit our store, you will be pleasantly surprised! It is bright, beautiful and inspiring.

At Glass Expressions we maintain a stock of competitively priced high quality glass art supplies and tools that we can ship to Canada and the US. We use the tools and products we sell and are actively creating glass artwork, so we can be of great help with any project you're involved with.
