Why Choose Ambassador?

Ambassador Transportation Corporation's goal is to provide the best ground and airport transportation service on Vancouver Island. All our drivers are professional and have a wealth of experience, which is put to use in all our services.

We offer shuttle services from Comox Airport to Comox Valley and surrounding areas, including Nanaimo and Tofino.

For those extra special occasions when you want to make a grand entrance and travel in style, we also provide limousine services.

With lots of activities all year round, you don't ever have to miss out. We will arrange to get you and your group there and home safely afterwards.

Serving our Community

Ambassador is owned and operated by residents of Vancouver Island. We are committed to our comunnity and we are always seeking ways to support local businesses.

To this end, we have partenered with restuarants, wineries and breweries to deliver exceptional tour experiences.
