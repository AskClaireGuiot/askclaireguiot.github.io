About Us

Glass Expressions is a retail outlet for a good variety of supplies, tools and glass.
If you can visit our store, you will be pleasantly surprised! It is bright, beautiful and inspiring.
At Glass Expressions we maintain a stock of competitively priced high quality glass art supplies
and tools that we can ship to Canada and the US.
We use the tools and products we sell and are actively creating glass artwork,
so we can be of great help with any project you're involved with.

Contact us for current prices and if you need information on our products.

Jan Lindstrom's gallery and studio, Jan's Glass by the Sea, is open right across the street.
Jan gladly accepts custom glass art orders or she will teach you the skills to create your own.
Please visit Jan's Glass by the Sea for more information on stained glass workshops.
